Quid Pro Quo


A quid pro quo attack is where an attacker is offering a benefit such as a service in return for access to sensitive information
It is often disguised as the target getting something they want whilst in actuality it's the attacker thats getting what they want.
For example, a fake IT help scenario in a business setting could be the attacker presenting as an IT help technician, whilst withholding the help required until the target provides a password or remote access to their device.

Ways to prevent a Quid Pro Quo attack

Ways to prevent a Quid Pro Quo attack include:
1. Verify if the contact is legitimate.
2. Don't provide any personal or financial information.
3. Take your time and think before acting.
