What is Social Engineering

o Social Engineering is an umbrella term referring to a wide range of actions by those with malicious intentions, through the psychological manipulation of the public, often resulting in breaches of personal and business security leaving user data vulnerable.
o A social engineering attack would commonly be referred to as a scam by the public.
o Social engineering attacks happen in stages.
o Firstly, the attacker will investigate either their targeted victim, either an individual or a business, and attempt to identify points of weakness that would allow them to mount an attack.
